# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation details.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.
